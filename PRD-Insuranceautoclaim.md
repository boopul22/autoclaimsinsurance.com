# 📄 PRD — Insuranceautoclaim.com Landing Page

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Insuranceautoclaim.com Landing Page
**Operated by:** Evolve Web Marketing Ltd (Company No. 15814738, England & Wales)
**Purpose:** A lead-generation landing page that connects car accident victims in the US with claims specialists. It captures user contact information and state, then routes them to a claims team.

---

## 2. Goals

- Communicate the core value proposition: fast, free, stress-free car accident claim assistance.
- Generate qualified leads via a contact/evaluation form.
- Drive phone call conversions via a prominently displayed phone number.
- Build trust through guarantees, social proof, and transparent process communication.

---

## 3. Target Audience

US-based individuals who have recently been in a car accident (non-fault), are looking for claim assistance, and may be unfamiliar with the legal or insurance process.
